Privacy Policy

Span — Countdown & Elapsed Time Tracker

Effective date: March 1, 2026

Overview

Span is designed with your privacy in mind. Your data stays on your device. We do not collect, track, or share any personal information.

Data Storage

All span data (event names, dates, and preferences) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up.

iCloud Backup

Span offers an optional manual iCloud backup feature. When you use it, your span data is saved to your personal iCloud Drive account. This data is managed entirely by Apple's iCloud infrastructure and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No cookies or web tracking
  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, or other device data

Third-Party Services

Span does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external service used is Apple's StoreKit for processing in-app purchases, which is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Notifications

Span may request permission to send local notifications to alert you about upcoming events. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers.

Children's Privacy

Span does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not collect information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
